Rationalized Hoarding

This page is an attempt at turning my dusty shelves into a library for friends and family. I figure if I'm hoarding, I might as well put it to some use.

"Dusty Shelves" might be the wrong term; I ran out of space a long time ago. My library consists of serveral bags left abandoned around the house.

It should go without saying that owning a book isn't an endorsement. There are plently on my shelf that I'm not fond of; a good portion has been purchased out of intruige.

If you'd like to discuss any book mention or have any recommendations, feel free to send me an email!
