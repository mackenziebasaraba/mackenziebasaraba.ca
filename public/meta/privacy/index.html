Privacy Policy

TLDR

This website is not logging any data, it does not use any scripts for tracking visitors, nor does it place any cookies on your computer.

This site and its owner do not collect any information on users. I have a thousand preventative measures to resist fingerprinting on my machine; it would be a bit hypocritical to turn around and collect user analytics. To be frank, beyond moral qualms, this is just an elaborate explanation to avoid admitting that I’m too much of a lazy bastard to configure and monitor a data system.

Logging

  • this access and error logs are disabled in nginx so there is no trace of visitors

Data Transfer

  • https protocol
  • encrypts all data between the browser and the server
  • network provider can see that you accessed my site

Cookies

This site does not deposit or read cookies; instead, I only use a small setting in local storage to retain your theme setting. This is solely for your convenience, as it allows you to maintain your preferred theme between pages. Clearing your browser cache will remove my site’s presence on your machine.

FranTech

This site is an nginx instance hosted by FranTech . To my knowledge, my host company doesn’t collect any information on client VPSs. If like Vultr, they change their policy, I will immediately look into changing hosts.
